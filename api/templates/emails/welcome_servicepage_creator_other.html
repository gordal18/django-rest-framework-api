What else can you do on UCROO?

Apart from being the one-stop-shop for all things related, you can also:

  • Share timetables with peers so you know who you've got class with and when they're free
  • Easily join class groups to collaborate on coursework with classmates in your unit
  • Create and join study groups to work on assignments, exams and areas of academic interest
  • Share, laugh and create with peers from across the university, as well as your school and year level
  • Chat with relevant peers, classmates, academics and services staff when they're online

We look forward to seeing you on UCROO!
