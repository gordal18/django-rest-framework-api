While you can expect your UCROO experience to be smooth sailing most of the time, it's worth knowing that we're always here to help should something go wrong.

If you are ever having trouble on UCROO, come across a glitch or have some feedback for us, you can access UCROO Support any time.

UCROO Support can be accessed via:

1. The cog icon in the top right corner of your account on a desktop

2. The menu bar on the UCROO iPhone or Android App

3. By visiting the UCROO Support Site

You can also lodge a support query at any time by emailing support@ucroo.com
