What is UCROO?

UCROO is a social learning network that encourages peer-to-peer learning online. In order to ensure that your students are learning effectively it helps to have academics involved.

What are the benefits for academics?

  1. Save time - avoid answering the same email over and over by answering questions on a platform where students are actively engaged
  2. Better student results - research suggests that social learning is a great way for students to learn. Our platform enables students to discuss course content at any time by asking each other questions, posting videos and uploading files
  3. Students like using UCROO - usability is crucial to the online learning experience which is why we have created a seamless interface
  4. Assessment reminders - create automatic reminders so students remember to submit assignments on time
