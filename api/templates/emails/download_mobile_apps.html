Having UCROO on your mobile puts your entire university community in your pocket and makes it easy to ask questions or access information when you are on the go.

Here are some of the benefits of having the mobile app:

Ask questions as they arise in your lecture or on the way home

Ask other students for help if you are lost on campus

Check your on campus bar as you walk out of class to see whether your connections and/or teachers are free to catch up
